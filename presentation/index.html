The Problem

Desk booking is broken

People mean to book, then forget — and teams end up scattered with no visibility of where anyone is sitting.

😬

"I'll book it later..."

Staff intend to reserve a desk but it slips their mind — leaving them scrambling on the day with no seat available.

👥

Where is my team sitting?

No visibility of where colleagues are — teams end up scattered across different floors with no easy way to coordinate.

📭

Booked but never shown up

Desks get reserved and then abandoned — wasting limited space and leaving others without options.

🔕

No advance warning

Nothing prompts staff to book ahead of time — reminders come too late, or not at all.

Our Solution

Introducing Spacio

A smarter desk booking experience built around how your team actually works — with visibility, proximity and proactive reminders built in from the start.

🗺️

Book Smarter

Visual, interactive floor plans make finding and reserving the right desk effortless — see real-time availability at a glance.

🤝

Stay Together

Smart suggestions automatically surface desks near your teammates — so your team sits together without extra effort.

🔔

Stay Reminded

Proactive Microsoft Teams notifications sent 2 weeks ahead — so nobody forgets to book and no desk goes to waste.

Key Features

Everything your team needs

🗺️

Interactive Floor Plan

Click any available desk on your real office layout. Colour-coded availability — green, amber, blue — at a glance.

🤝

Smart Team Suggestions

Automatically recommends available desks closest to where your team is already sitting that day.

⚡

Auto-Release

Didn't check in? Spacio prompts you and auto-releases the desk — keeping space available for others.

💬

Teams Reminders

Sends a booking reminder to your Microsoft Teams channel 2 weeks ahead, with a direct "Book now" link.

🔧

Admin Controls

Drag-to-place floor plan editor, force-release, advance booking management and full occupancy overview.

🔒

Privacy Mode

Book privately to hide your name from colleagues. Admins always retain full visibility for workspace management.
